About Me

I have extensive experience in both front-end and back-end development as a web developer. I discovered my passion for optimizing and automating business processes while working as an IT support engineer. To enhance my skills, I took it upon myself to attend courses and earn certifications in different programming languages and automation tools like Python, SQL, PowerShell, and Ansible

Over time, my knowledge and skills expanded, and I was able to undertake complex projects like integrating various systems, automating manual processes, and developing customized applications, to building web applications both front and back end. I also collaborated with other developers and engineers to create efficient and user-friendly systems.

Through my efforts, I was promoted to an IT Manager role where I focused on identifying opportunities for automation and digitalization of business processes to enhance efficiency and cut operational costs. I have a comprehensive understanding of the IT infrastructure landscape, from on-premise to cloud-based solutions like AWS. My experience in both IT engineering and web application development has given me a unique perspective on how technology can be leveraged to drive business growth.
