About Me

Jen Ho is a full-stack web developer with over 2 years of experience building dynamic, responsive websites and web applications. She has a passion for front-end development and enjoys creating beautiful, intuitive user interfaces using the latest web technologies.

Jen Ho's expertise in back-end development is equally impressive, with a strong command of programming languages such as Python, Ruby, and JavaScript. She has experience building and maintaining databases, designing APIs, and integrating with third-party services.

In her previous role at a large e-commerce company, Jen Ho was responsible for leading the development of a custom content management system that streamlined the content creation process for over 50 editors and writers. She also spearheaded the company's transition to a headless CMS, improving site performance and reducing page load times by 40%.

In her free time, Jen Ho enjoys attending hackathons, contributing to open-source projects, and mentoring aspiring web developers. She is passionate about promoting diversity and inclusion in tech and is an active member of several women in tech communities.
